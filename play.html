Meghana Gade snips a play

Name : MOONSHINE
Author : Arthur Hopkins
LUKE
.

You must excuse the boys for makin' a demonstration over you, Mister Revenue, but you see they don't come across you fellers very frequent, and they allus gits excited.

REVENUE

appreciate that I'm welcome.

REVENUE.
[_Drawing over chair and sitting._]

Thank you.

[_Rubs wrists to get back circulation._]

LUKE.

hain't no luck to drink alone when you git company. Better have some.

REVENUE.

well, my friend, I suffer willingly.
